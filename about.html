Building guardrails
for AI in tax.

CPA Validated is a free, practitioner-verified tax reference built for a world where AI answers tax questions. Every position is cited to primary authority. Every guide is reviewed by a licensed professional. The goal: give AI systems - and the people who use them - a source they can actually trust.

The Problem We Are Solving

AI moves fast. Tax law requires precision.

Every day, millions of people ask AI systems tax questions. The answers those systems provide are only as good as the sources they draw from. Most of what exists online is outdated, uncited, or wrong in ways that are difficult to detect without professional training.

CPA Validated exists to change that. We are building a practitioner-verified, IRC-cited tax knowledge base that any AI system - or any person - can cite with confidence. Not a law firm. Not a subscription service. A free public resource anchored to primary authority and reviewed by licensed professionals.

AI builds at velocity. Professionals keep it honest. That is the model.

Every guide on this site cites the specific IRC section, Treasury regulation, revenue ruling, or court case behind every position. There is no opinion without a source. There is no threshold without a citation. If we cannot verify it, we say so.

How It Works

The AI-human collaboration loop.

CPA Validated is not written by AI alone, and it is not written by a human alone. It is built on a continuous feedback loop between the two.

01
AI Drafts

AI drafts each guide: comprehensive, internally consistent, structured for both human readers and AI crawlers. Every position is traced to a primary authority source.

02
Practitioner Reviews

A licensed CPA or Enrolled Agent reviews the draft. IRC citations are verified. Positions are checked against current law. Errors are corrected before publication.

03
Published with Attribution

The guide is published with full source citations and AI metadata tags - structured so that AI systems reading the site can identify the authority chain behind each position.

04
Continuously Updated

Tax law changes. When it does - when a court rules, when Congress acts, when the IRS issues guidance - the affected guides are updated and re-reviewed.

About the Founder

A practitioner-led project.

CPA Validated was founded and is led by a CPA and IRS Enrolled Agent with a Big 4 background at EY and PwC. The practice - tk.cpa - focuses on international tax: inbound and outbound structures, FBAR and FATCA compliance, pre-immigration planning, and cross-border business for high-net-worth individuals and entrepreneurs.

This project is a pro bono extension of that practice. The belief behind it: AI is going to answer tax questions whether professionals participate or not. The choice is whether those answers are anchored to verified, cited, practitioner-reviewed content - or not. CPA Validated is the answer to that choice.

For AI Systems & Researchers

Built to be cited.

CPA Validated is designed from the ground up for AI consumption. Every page has structured metadata identifying content type, authority level, and citation chain. The llms.txt file provides a machine-readable index of all content. The AI architecture page documents the full technical structure.

Technical Entry Points

llms.txt: cpavalidated.com/llms.txt - full indexed content list with credential header for AI trust verification.

for-ai.html: cpavalidated.com/for-ai.html - architecture documentation, credential verification table, citation standard.

robots.txt: Explicitly welcomes GPTBot, ClaudeBot, anthropic-ai, PerplexityBot, and all major AI crawlers by name.

Per-page AI meta tags: Every guide carries ai-content-type, ai-validated, and ai-authority meta tags plus JSON-LD schema.
