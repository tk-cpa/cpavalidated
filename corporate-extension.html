Universal rule: An extension of time to FILE never extends the time to PAY. Tax owed is due by the original due date in all jurisdictions. Interest and failure-to-pay penalties accrue from the original due date on any unpaid balance. Federal Form 7004 provides an automatic 6-month extension for calendar-year C-Corps: April 15 → October 15.
