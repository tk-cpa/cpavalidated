2

Bonus Depreciation - QIP Only

ℹ
QIP is eligible for bonus depreciation. Residential and commercial real property are NOT eligible for bonus depreciation - they use straight-line only. IRC §168(k)(2)(A)(i) specifically excludes property with MACRS recovery periods of 27.5 or 39 years. QIP (15-year property) is eligible. Post-OBBBA (P.L. 119-21): 100% bonus depreciation is permanent for property acquired and placed in service after January 19, 2025. Property under a written binding contract dated before 1/20/2025 remains under the TCJA phase-down (40% for 2025).
