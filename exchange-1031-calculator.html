Identification Deadline
45
days from closing of relinquished property to identify replacement properties in writing. No exceptions. IRC 1031(a)(3)(A). Miss this: no exchange.
Closing Deadline
180
days from closing of relinquished property (or tax return due date if earlier) to close on replacement property. IRC 1031(a)(3)(B). These are calendar days, not business days.

Relinquished Property

Gross sale price of the property being sold (relinquished)
Original cost + improvements - accumulated depreciation. This is NOT the original purchase price if you have depreciated the property.
Total depreciation taken over the holding period. Subject to 25% unrecaptured Section 1250 gain on sale (even in a successful 1031 exchange, this transfers to replacement property).
Commissions, closing costs, legal fees, title insurance. Reduce the amount realized.
Outstanding mortgage balance. Debt relief = boot unless replaced with equal or greater debt on replacement property. IRC 1031(b).
2

Replacement Property

Price of replacement property. Must be equal or greater than net sale price to avoid boot.
New debt on replacement property. Offsets debt relief from relinquished property. If new debt is less than old debt, the difference is mortgage boot.
