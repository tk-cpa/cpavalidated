Basic Information

Employees cannot deduct home office expenses after TCJA (2018-2025, now permanent under OBBBA). The home office deduction for W-2 employees was eliminated by the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act as a miscellaneous itemized deduction. Only self-employed individuals, sole proprietors (Schedule C), farmers (Schedule F), and partners with unreimbursed partnership expenses may claim this deduction. If you are a W-2 employee, this calculator does not apply to you. IRC §67(g).
