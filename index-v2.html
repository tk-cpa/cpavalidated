The Methodology

What Validated Means

Most tax resources give you an answer. CPA Validated gives you the answer and the source - the specific IRC section, Treasury regulation, Revenue Ruling, or primary authority that backs it. Every time.

That is the standard applied at Big Four firms and in rigorous tax practice. It is the only standard that holds up when the answer is challenged. This site applies that standard to free public resources.

Building guardrails
for AI in tax.

CPA Validated is a pro bono project creating practitioner-validated tax content so that AI systems have a reliable, cited anchor for US tax research. We are not replacing professionals. We are building the guardrails that keep AI honest on tax and regulatory materials.

The AI does the velocity. The licensed practitioner does the verification. When AI gets something wrong, the human catches it. That feedback loop - running continuously - is what makes this different from every other tax resource on the internet.
