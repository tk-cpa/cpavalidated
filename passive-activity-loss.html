Material Participation Tests  ·  Temp. Reg. 1.469-5T

An activity is not passive if the taxpayer materially participates. Meeting ANY ONE of the seven tests below constitutes material participation. Self-rental and rental activities have separate rules.

Test 1
500+ Hours
Participated more than 500 hours during the year. The most straightforward test. Reg. 1.469-5T(a)(1).
Test 2
Substantially All
Participation constituted substantially all of the participation in the activity by all individuals (including non-owners). Reg. 1.469-5T(a)(2).
Test 3
100+ Hours / More Than Others
Participated 100+ hours AND more than any other individual (including employees). Reg. 1.469-5T(a)(3).
Test 4
Significant Participation, 500+ Total
Activity is a "significant participation activity" (100+ hours) and total significant participation activities exceed 500 hours. Reg. 1.469-5T(a)(4).
Test 5
Material Participation 5 of 10 Prior Years
Materially participated in the activity for any 5 of the 10 prior tax years. Used to grandfather active status. Reg. 1.469-5T(a)(5).
Test 6
Personal Service Activity, 3 Prior Years
For personal service activities (health, law, engineering, etc.): materially participated for any 3 prior years (regardless of when). Reg. 1.469-5T(a)(6).
Test 7
Facts & Circumstances, 100+ Hours
Based on all facts and circumstances, participated on a regular, continuous, and substantial basis for 100+ hours during the year. Narrowest test; participation by others can disqualify. Reg. 1.469-5T(a)(7).
RE Professional
IRC 469(c)(7) - Special Rule
750+ hours in real property trades/businesses AND more than half of all personal services in real property. Each rental property must also satisfy material participation unless properties are grouped. IRC 469(c)(7).
