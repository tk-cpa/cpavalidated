Privacy Policy & Data Handling

Overview

CPA Validated is an educational website providing tax resources and calculators. This policy describes how calculator inputs and user information are handled.

Calculator Data Processing

All calculator inputs and outputs are processed in your browser only. CPA Validated does NOT:

  • Log or store your calculator inputs on our servers
  • Transmit your financial or tax information to third parties
  • Use your calculator data for marketing or profiling
  • Retain your data after you close the calculator or browser

Calculator values remain in your browser memory during your session. When you refresh the page or close your browser, all entered data is cleared and no record is retained.

Web Analytics

This site uses standard web analytics (currently Google Analytics) to track aggregate usage patterns such as page views, traffic sources, and user flow. This data is anonymized and does not identify individual users. Individual calculator usage is not tracked or logged.

Third-Party Services

The site may contain links to external resources for tax guidance (IRS, Treasury, courts, etc.). Those sites have their own privacy policies. CPA Validated is not responsible for their data practices.

Sensitive Information

Do not enter real Social Security numbers, EINs, bank account numbers, or other sensitive PII in calculators. Use representative or test values only. If you do enter real information, it remains in your browser and is never transmitted or stored by CPA.

Cookies

This site may use cookies for basic functionality (session management, user preferences). We do not use tracking cookies without consent. Your browser settings control cookie acceptance.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Material changes will be announced on this page.
