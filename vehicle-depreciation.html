Depreciation Elections

§179 limit for passenger autos and SUVs is subject to §280F luxury auto caps regardless of the overall §179 limit ($2.5M post-OBBBA, indexed for inflation, with $4M phaseout). Heavy trucks and vans (non-SUV >6,000 lbs) are not subject to the §280F caps and can use the full §179 amount.
