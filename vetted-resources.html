Primary Legal Authority — United States
US Congress / Government
Internal Revenue Code — Cornell LII
Free
The complete Internal Revenue Code (Title 26, USC), maintained and updated by the Legal Information Institute at Cornell Law School. The most accessible free version of primary US tax law. Always cite to the IRC section, not to summaries of it.
law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26
Open Resource
Internal Revenue Service
IRS.gov — Official IRS Website
Official
Primary source for IRS publications, Revenue Procedures, Revenue Rulings, notices, forms, and instructions. Revenue Rulings and Rev. Procs. published in the Internal Revenue Bulletin are primary authority under Circular 230.
irs.gov
Open Resource
US Department of Treasury
Treasury Regulations — eCFR
Free
The Electronic Code of Federal Regulations (eCFR), Title 26. Treasury Regulations have the force of law and are primary authority under Circular 230. Treas. Reg. §1.XXX corresponds to IRC §XXX. Always check for proposed vs. final regulations.
ecfr.gov/current/title-26
Open Resource
Financial Crimes Enforcement Network
FinCEN — FBAR / BSA Resources
Official
Official source for FBAR (FinCEN Form 114) filing, Bank Secrecy Act guidance, and anti-money laundering regulations. FBAR is filed through the BSA E-Filing System linked here. Primary source for 31 USC §5314 compliance.
fincen.gov
Open Resource
Internal Revenue Service
IRS Yearly Average Exchange Rates
Official
The only IRS-approved source for yearly average currency exchange rates used in FBAR, FATCA, and international tax return preparation. Required for converting foreign account values and income to USD for US tax reporting purposes.
irs.gov/individuals/international-taxpayers/yearly-average-currency-exchange-rates
Open Resource
US Congress
Congress.gov — Primary Legislation
Official
Official source for Public Laws, bill text, and legislative history. Essential for tracing the legislative intent behind IRC provisions. OBBBA (P.L. 119-21), TCJA (P.L. 115-97), CARES Act (P.L. 116-136) all available here in full text.
congress.gov
Open Resource
International Tax Research
PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC)
Worldwide Tax Summaries Online
Free
Corporate and individual tax rates, rules, and summaries for 150+ territories worldwide. Written and updated regularly by local PwC tax specialists. The most comprehensive free international tax reference available. Covers CIT rates, VAT rates, withholding taxes, due dates, and key tax administration rules by country.
taxsummaries.pwc.com
Open Resource
OECD
Corporate Tax Statistics Database
Free
The OECD's flagship corporate tax statistics publication covering 160+ countries. Statutory CIT rates, effective tax rates, R&D incentives, and country-by-country reporting data. Key resource for Pillar Two / global minimum tax analysis and transfer pricing benchmarking. Updated annually.
oecd.org/en/data/datasets/corporate-income-tax-rates-database.html
Open Resource
OECD
OECD Tax Policy & Statistics
Official
OECD's central hub for tax policy analysis, revenue statistics, and international tax frameworks including BEPS Action Plans, Pillar One, Pillar Two, and the Inclusive Framework. Primary source for understanding the OECD/G20 global tax architecture.
oecd.org/tax
Open Resource
Ernst & Young (EY)
EY Worldwide Corporate Tax Guide
Free
EY's annual guide to corporate tax rules in 160+ jurisdictions. Companion to the PwC Worldwide Tax Summaries - useful for cross-referencing and for country-specific detail on corporate tax structures, group relief, and controlled foreign company rules.
ey.com/en_gl/tax-guides
Open Resource
IRS
US Income Tax Treaties — Full Text
Official
Full text of all US income tax treaties currently in force, published by the IRS. Essential for any cross-border tax position. Treaty articles override domestic law in most cases. Always check the specific treaty article before relying on a general summary.
irs.gov/businesses/international-businesses/united-states-income-tax-treaties-a-to-z
Open Resource
KPMG
KPMG Corporate Tax Rate Table
Free
KPMG's annual corporate tax rate table comparing statutory rates across jurisdictions. Useful for quick country-level rate lookups and historical rate comparisons. Published annually. Cross-reference with PwC and OECD for accuracy.
kpmg.com/us/en/home/insights/2021/09/tnf-kpmg-corporate-tax-rates-table.html
Open Resource
Official Government Tax Authorities
United Kingdom
HMRC — HM Revenue & Customs
Official
UK primary tax authority. Source for UK income tax, corporation tax, VAT, PAYE, National Insurance, and capital gains rules. Essential for US-UK cross-border situations, treaty positions, and pension planning for UK-connected individuals.
gov.uk/government/organisations/hm-revenue-customs
Open Resource
Canada
CRA — Canada Revenue Agency
Official
Canadian primary tax authority. Source for Canadian income tax, RRSP/RRIF rules, GST/HST, and treaty positions. Critical for US-Canada cross-border planning, RRSP deferral elections, and dual-status taxpayer situations.
canada.ca/en/revenue-agency.html
Open Resource
Israel
Israel Tax Authority (ITA)
Official
Israeli primary tax authority. Source for Israeli income tax, capital gains, Keren Hishtalmut rules, and reporting obligations. Relevant for the significant US-Israel cross-border tax practice community given the US-Israel tax treaty and large Israeli-American population.
taxes.gov.il/English
Open Resource
Germany
Bundeszentralamt für Steuern (BZSt)
Official
German Federal Central Tax Office. Primary source for German tax law, withholding tax refund applications, and VAT registrations. Relevant for US-Germany cross-border situations, German pension reporting, and treaty applications.
bzst.de/EN
Open Resource
European Union
EUR-Lex — EU Tax Law
Official
Official access to European Union law including VAT directives, DAC6 mandatory disclosure rules, ATAD (Anti-Tax Avoidance Directives), and Pillar Two directives. Essential for practitioners with EU-connected clients.
eur-lex.europa.eu
Open Resource
Puerto Rico
Hacienda — Puerto Rico Treasury
Official
Puerto Rico Department of Treasury. Official source for Act 60 (Individual Investors, Export Services), tax decree applications, and Puerto Rico income tax. Primary authority for PR tax planning, including the December 31, 2026 deadline for 0% Act 60 rate.
hacienda.pr.gov
Open Resource
State & Local Tax Research
Tax Foundation
State Tax Rates & Rankings
Free
The definitive independent source for state income tax, corporate tax, sales tax, and property tax data. The State Business Tax Climate Index and state-by-state rate tables are used by practitioners, academics, and policymakers. Updated annually. Nonpartisan.
taxfoundation.org/topics/state-taxes
Open Resource
Tax Foundation
Corporate Tax Rates Around the World
Free
Annual publication covering statutory corporate income tax rates in 180+ jurisdictions. The dataset is published in full and is frequently cited in academic and policy research. Sourced from PwC, OECD, KPMG, and EY data with Tax Foundation verification.
taxfoundation.org/publications/corporate-tax-rates-around-the-world
Open Resource
Avalara
Economic Nexus Map & Thresholds
Free
State-by-state economic nexus thresholds and marketplace facilitator laws following South Dakota v. Wayfair (2018). Updated regularly as states enact or modify rules. Essential for SaaS companies, e-commerce businesses, and any company with multistate sales activity.
avalara.com/us/en/learn/sales-tax/economic-nexus.html
Open Resource
TaxJar
SaaS Sales Tax Guide by State
Free
State-by-state guide to SaaS taxability - which states tax software-as-a-service, which exempt it, and which treat it as mixed. Essential reference for SaaS companies navigating sales tax compliance. Updated as state rules change.
taxjar.com/sales-tax/saas
Open Resource
Professional Research Tools
Google Scholar
Tax Court & Federal Court Decisions
Free
Free access to US Tax Court memoranda, circuit court decisions, and Supreme Court opinions. Search by IRC section, taxpayer name, or legal concept. Not a substitute for a professional tax research platform but useful for case-finding and case law verification.
scholar.google.com
Open Resource
US Tax Court
Tax Court Opinions — Official
Official
Official source for US Tax Court opinions, including regular decisions (precedential) and memorandum decisions. Regular Tax Court decisions are binding precedent; memo decisions are persuasive. Free, searchable, updated daily.
ustaxcourt.gov/USTCInOP/OpinionSearch.aspx
Open Resource
IRS
IRS Internal Revenue Manual (IRM)
Official
The IRS's own internal procedures manual. While not binding on taxpayers, the IRM reveals how IRS agents are instructed to handle examinations, penalty assessments, offer-in-compromise, and collections. Invaluable for understanding IRS procedure.
irs.gov/irm
Open Resource
Oxford University (CBT)
Oxford Tax Database
Free
The Centre for Business Taxation at Oxford's Saïd Business School maintains a comprehensive multi-country tax database covering 43 OECD and G20 countries from 1979 onward. Useful for academic-grade research on effective tax rates and historical rate comparisons.
oxfordtax.sbs.ox.ac.uk/cbt-tax-database
