Primary US Legal Authority6 resources
Cornell Law / US Congress
Internal Revenue Code (Title 26)
Free
The complete IRC maintained by Cornell LII. Most accessible free version of primary US tax law. Always cite to the IRC section, not to summaries of it.
law.cornell.edu/uscode/text/26
Open
US Treasury / eCFR
Treasury Regulations (Title 26 CFR)
Free
Electronic CFR, Title 26. Treasury Regulations have the force of law and are primary authority under Circular 230. Always check proposed vs. final status.
ecfr.gov/current/title-26
Open
Internal Revenue Service
IRS.gov - Primary IRS Source
Official
Revenue Procedures, Revenue Rulings, Notices, forms, and instructions. Rev. Rulings and Rev. Procs. in the Internal Revenue Bulletin are primary authority under Circular 230.
irs.gov
Open
IRS
Yearly Average Currency Exchange Rates
Official
The only IRS-approved source for yearly average exchange rates used in FBAR, FATCA, and international return preparation. Required for USD conversion of foreign account values.
irs.gov/.../yearly-average-currency-exchange-rates
Open
US Tax Court
Tax Court Opinions - Official
Official
Official source for Tax Court opinions. Regular decisions are binding precedent; memoranda decisions are persuasive. Free, searchable, updated daily.
ustaxcourt.gov
Open
IRS
IRS Internal Revenue Manual (IRM)
Official
The IRS's own internal procedures manual. Reveals how IRS agents are instructed to conduct examinations, assess penalties, and handle collections. Not binding on taxpayers but invaluable for understanding IRS procedure.
irs.gov/irm
Open
Big Four International Research Tools4 resources
PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC)
Worldwide Tax Summaries Online
Free
The gold standard for international tax reference. Corporate and individual tax rates, withholding taxes, VAT rates, due dates, and key rules for 150+ territories worldwide. Written and updated by local PwC specialists in each country. Quick Charts give territory-by-territory rate comparisons at a glance. The first place to look for any cross-border tax question.
taxsummaries.pwc.com
Open
Ernst & Young (EY)
EY Worldwide Tax and Transaction Guides
Free
EY's annual tax guides covering 160+ jurisdictions: Corporate Tax Guide, Individual Tax Guide, VAT/GST Guide, Transfer Pricing Guide. Essential companion to PwC Worldwide Tax Summaries for cross-referencing and country-specific depth on CFC rules, group relief, and treaty positions.
ey.com/en_gl/tax-guides
Open
KPMG
KPMG Corporate Tax Rate Table
Free
KPMG's annual statutory corporate tax rate comparison across 170+ jurisdictions. Quick country-level rate lookups and historical trend analysis. Cross-reference with PwC and OECD for accuracy. Covers current year and multi-year historical rates.
kpmg.com - Corporate Tax Rates Table
Open
Deloitte
Deloitte International Tax Source
Free
Deloitte's country-by-country tax highlight guides covering corporate tax, individual tax, withholding taxes, and indirect taxes for 150+ countries. Valuable where PwC or EY coverage is thin, particularly for emerging markets and less-covered jurisdictions.
dits.deloitte.com
Open
Tax Foundation - State & Federal Data5 resources
Tax Foundation
2026 State Income Tax Rates & Brackets
Free
Definitive annual reference for state individual income tax rates, brackets, standard deductions, and personal exemptions for all 50 states. Updated for 2026 including OBBBA conformity analysis. Nonpartisan, data-driven, primary practitioner reference.
taxfoundation.org/data/all/state/state-income-tax-rates-2026/
Open
Tax Foundation
2026 Federal Tax Brackets & Parameters
Free
Federal income tax brackets, standard deductions, AMT exemptions, estate/gift tax parameters for 2026. Includes OBBBA changes. Sourced from IRS Rev. Proc. 2025-32. Fastest verified reference for 2026 federal thresholds.
taxfoundation.org/data/all/federal/2026-tax-brackets/
Open
Tax Foundation
State Tax Competitiveness Index 2026
Free
Annual ranking of all 50 states on 150+ variables: corporate taxes, individual income taxes, sales and excise taxes, property taxes, unemployment insurance. Essential for state tax planning and business location analysis.
taxfoundation.org/statetaxindex/
Open
Tax Foundation
Corporate Tax Rates Around the World
Free
Annual publication covering statutory CIT rates in 180+ jurisdictions. Full dataset published. Sourced from PwC, OECD, KPMG, and EY data. Widely cited in academic and policy research.
taxfoundation.org/publications/corporate-tax-rates-around-the-world
Open
Tax Foundation
Facts & Figures 2026 - State Tax Data
Free
One-stop reference comparing all 50 states on 40+ tax measures: rates, collections, burdens, property taxes, sales taxes. Interactive tool and downloadable PDF. Published annually since 1941.
taxfoundation.org/data/all/state/2026-state-tax-data/
Open
OECD & International Policy4 resources
OECD
Corporate Tax Statistics Database
Free
OECD's flagship corporate tax publication covering 160+ countries. Statutory CIT rates, effective tax rates, R&D incentives, and country-by-country reporting data. Key for Pillar Two / global minimum tax analysis. Updated annually.
oecd.org/en/data/datasets/corporate-income-tax-rates-database.html
Open
OECD
OECD Tax Policy & BEPS
Free
Central hub for BEPS Action Plans, Pillar One, Pillar Two, the Inclusive Framework, and the global minimum tax. Primary source for understanding the international tax architecture affecting multinationals and HNW individuals with cross-border structures.
oecd.org/tax
Open
IRS
US Income Tax Treaties - Full Text
Official
Full text of all US income tax treaties currently in force. Treaty articles override domestic law in most cases. Always read the specific treaty article - not a summary - before relying on a treaty position.
irs.gov/businesses/international-businesses/united-states-income-tax-treaties-a-to-z
Open
European Union / EUR-Lex
EU Tax Law & Directives
Official
Official EU law including VAT Directives, DAC6 mandatory disclosure rules, ATAD (Anti-Tax Avoidance Directives), and Pillar Two Directive. Essential for practitioners with EU-connected clients or entities.
eur-lex.europa.eu
Open
Sales Tax, Nexus & SaaS Compliance4 resources
Avalara
State-by-State Economic Nexus Guide
Free
Most comprehensive free reference for post-Wayfair economic nexus thresholds by state. Covers all 50 states with revenue and transaction thresholds, included transaction types, and marketplace facilitator rules. Updated as states change rules (e.g., Illinois dropped 200-transaction threshold January 2026).
avalara.com/us/en/learn/guides/state-by-state-guide-economic-nexus-laws.html
Open
TaxJar
SaaS Sales Tax Taxability Guide
Free
State-by-state guide to SaaS taxability - which states tax software-as-a-service, which exempt it, and which apply mixed rules based on delivery method or access model. The primary free reference for SaaS companies navigating sales tax. Updated as state rules change.
taxjar.com/sales-tax/saas
Open
Avalara
Economic Nexus Overview & Risk Assessment
Free
Explanatory guide to economic nexus rules post-South Dakota v. Wayfair (2018), physical presence nexus, and state income tax triggers. Includes free multi-state nexus risk assessment tool.
avalara.com/us/en/learn/nexus.html
Open
Streamlined Sales Tax
SST Registration System
Free
Simultaneous registration in 24 SST member states through one system. Essential for remote sellers managing multi-state compliance. Free registration for sellers using certified SST software.
streamlinedsalestax.org
Open

Professional Research & Ethics4 resources
Google Scholar
Tax Court & Federal Court Decisions
Free
Free access to US Tax Court memoranda, circuit court decisions, and Supreme Court opinions. Search by IRC section, taxpayer name, or legal concept. Good for case-finding; not a substitute for a professional research platform.
scholar.google.com
Open
Oxford University (CBT)
Oxford Centre for Business Taxation DB
Free
Multi-country effective tax rate database covering 43 OECD and G20 countries from 1979 onward. Valuable for academic-grade research on effective tax rates, historical comparisons, and transfer pricing benchmarking.
oxfordtax.sbs.ox.ac.uk/cbt-tax-database
Open
AICPA
Statements on Standards for Tax Services
Free
The SSTS are the professional standards governing CPA tax practice. Circular 230's professional complement from the AICPA. Every CPA providing tax services is subject to these standards.
aicpa-cima.com/topic/tax
Open
IRS / US Treasury
Circular 230 - Practice Before the IRS
Official
The regulations governing practice before the IRS for CPAs, attorneys, and enrolled agents. Primary authority for practitioner conduct, ethics, and standards of practice. Required reading for any tax professional.
irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/pcir230.pdf
