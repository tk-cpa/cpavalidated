Free Calculators & Validators - Federal Government8 resources
IRS
Tax Withholding Estimator
Official
The official IRS tool for adjusting Form W-4 withholding. Walks through filing status, income, deductions, credits. Generates a pre-filled W-4. The most authoritative withholding calculator available - directly from the source.
apps.irs.gov/app/tax-withholding-estimator
Open
IRS
Sales Tax Deduction Calculator
Official
Optional sales tax tables for taxpayers itemizing on Schedule A who deduct sales tax instead of state income tax. Uses ZIP code, family size, and income to estimate without receipts. Useful for low/no income tax states (FL, TX, WA, NV).
apps.irs.gov/app/stdc
Open
IRS
Interactive Tax Assistant (ITA)
Official
A decision-tree tool answering common tax questions. Filing requirements, dependents, credits, deductions, retirement distributions. Each answer cites the relevant statute or publication. Useful for client-facing FAQ-style questions.
irs.gov/help/ita
Open
IRS
EITC Assistant
Official
Determines eligibility and approximate amount of the Earned Income Tax Credit. Available in English and Spanish. Useful for taxpayers near the income threshold or with complex household situations.
irs.gov/.../use-the-eitc-assistant
Open
IRS
RMD Worksheets & Tables
Official
Official Required Minimum Distribution worksheets and the Uniform Lifetime Table. The only authoritative source for RMD calculations. SECURE 2.0 raised the age to 73 (then 75 in 2033).
irs.gov/.../retirement-topics-rmds
Open

Social Security retirement benefit estimator. Quick three-input version (date of birth, current earnings, retirement age). For higher precision, the my Social Security account uses actual earnings history.
ssa.gov/oact/quickcalc

More detailed retirement, disability, and survivor benefit calculator. Allows custom earnings histories and what-if scenarios. The authoritative tool for retirement planning involving Social Security.

FL Dept. of Revenue
Official
Florida has no personal income tax but has corporate income tax (5.5%), sales tax, documentary stamp tax, and intangible tax. Property tax is administered locally. Critical for Florida-relocation analysis and FL-based businesses.
floridarevenue.com

Texas
TX Comptroller
Official
No personal income tax, but franchise tax on businesses, sales/use tax, property tax administered locally. Online sales tax rate locator and franchise tax filing tools. Essential for TX-based and TX-relocating businesses.
comptroller.texas.gov
Open
Illinois
IL Dept. of Revenue
Official
Illinois personal (4.95% flat), corporate, sales tax, and franchise tax. MyTax Illinois portal for filing and payment. Estate tax exemption $4M (2026, indexed). Critical for IL residents and IL-source income.
tax.illinois.gov
Open
Massachusetts
MassTaxConnect (DOR)
Official
MA personal income tax (5% on most income, 4% surcharge on income over $1M effective 2023), corporate excise tax, sales/use tax. The "Millionaire's Tax" calculator and MA-source income rules.
mass.gov/orgs/massachusetts-department-of-revenue
Open
New Jersey
NJ Division of Taxation
Official
NJ personal income tax, corporate business tax (CBT), sales tax. NJ has its own depreciation rules and decouples from federal bonus depreciation - state addback workpapers are required. Essential for NY/NJ tristate situations.
nj.gov/treasury/taxation
Open
Pennsylvania
PA Dept. of Revenue
Official
PA personal (3.07% flat), corporate (CNIT, reduced to 4.99% by 2031), sales tax, and complex local Earned Income Tax (EIT). Pennsylvania has unusual conformity rules - state-specific addbacks for many federal items.
revenue.pa.gov

IRS Compliance & Practitioner Procedures5 resources
IRS / US Treasury
IRS Streamlined Filing Compliance Procedures
Official
The official IRS page for the Streamlined Foreign Offshore Procedure (SFOP) and Streamlined Domestic Offshore Procedure (SDOP). The primary amnesty path for non-willful FBAR and foreign reporting failures. Read the actual IRS page, not practitioner summaries - the certification language matters. Directly relevant to pre-immigration planning and late-filer remediation.
irs.gov/compliance/streamlined-filing-compliance-procedures
Open
IRS Criminal Investigation
IRS Voluntary Disclosure Practice (VDP)
Official
The current IRS Voluntary Disclosure Practice replaced the old OVDP (which closed in September 2018). VDP is available for willful violations. Requires pre-clearance, a narrative, amended returns, and negotiated penalties. Distinct from Streamlined - VDP is for willful conduct, Streamlined is for non-willful. Practitioners handling either must know both pages.
irs.gov/compliance/criminal-investigation
Open
IRS / National Taxpayer Advocate
Taxpayer Advocate Service (TAS)
Official
The IRS's independent advocate for taxpayers. The Annual Report to Congress is essential reading - it identifies the most serious IRS problems and audit priorities each year. TAS intervenes when normal IRS channels fail. The NTA blog is a practitioner-grade commentary on emerging IRS procedures and taxpayer rights issues.
taxpayeradvocate.irs.gov
Open
FinCEN / US Treasury
BSA E-Filing System (FBAR Filing Portal)
Official
The actual portal where FBAR (FinCEN Form 114) is filed. Distinct from FinCEN.gov. This is the direct filing link practitioners need for BSA E-Filing. FBAR is not filed with the IRS - it is filed directly with FinCEN through this system. Deadline: April 15 with automatic extension to October 15.
bsaefiling.fincen.treas.gov
Open
TIGTA / US Treasury
Treasury Inspector General for Tax Administration
Official
TIGTA audit reports reveal IRS enforcement priorities, systemic failures, and compliance data. The reports are public and regularly cover FBAR enforcement, foreign account compliance, passport revocation under IRC 7345, and EIN abuse. Required reading for practitioners tracking where IRS enforcement resources are actually being deployed.
tigta.gov
Open
Sanctions, OFAC & Financial Crimes2 resources
OFAC / US Treasury
Office of Foreign Assets Control - Sanctions Lists
Official
The Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) list and consolidated sanctions programs. Post-2022, OFAC compliance is a threshold issue for any practitioner with Russian-connected clients, cross-border wire transfers, or foreign entity representations. The SDN search tool is free. Failure to screen is not a defense - and penalties are strict liability. Know before you engage.
ofac.treasury.gov
Open
FinCEN / US Treasury
FinCEN - Anti-Money Laundering & BSA
Official
Primary source for Bank Secrecy Act regulations, beneficial ownership rules (CTA), and AML guidance. The Corporate Transparency Act (CTA) beneficial ownership reporting - though enforcement is currently suspended pending litigation - originated here. Any practitioner with entity clients needs to monitor this page for status updates.
fincen.gov
